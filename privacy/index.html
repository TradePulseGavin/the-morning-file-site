Overview

The Morning File is a short-form current-events commentary brand. This policy explains how information is handled when an authorized account owner connects TikTok to our publishing workflow or visits this website.

Information we process

When TikTok authorization is used, we may receive basic account information supplied by TikTok, such as an account identifier, display name, and avatar. We also process authorization tokens and publishing status information needed to upload or publish videos to the authorized account.

This website may receive routine technical request data, such as IP address, browser type, device type, timestamps, and diagnostic logs, through its hosting provider. We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information through this site.

How information is used

  • To authenticate an account owner and maintain an authorized connection.
  • To upload, publish, and check the status of requested video posts.
  • To protect the workflow, diagnose errors, and prevent unauthorized use.
  • To comply with platform rules and applicable legal obligations.

Sharing and sales

We do not sell personal information. Information is shared only with service providers needed to host the site, operate the publishing workflow, or connect to TikTok, and when disclosure is required by law or necessary to protect rights and security.

Retention and security

Authorization tokens and related operational records are kept only for as long as needed to maintain the connection, operate the workflow, resolve problems, and meet legal obligations. Reasonable administrative and technical safeguards are used, but no online system can be guaranteed completely secure.
